More About the Artifacts

This collection showcases the rich heritage of the tribal communities, featuring artifacts that have been preserved for centuries. These objects offer a glimpse into the cultural and historical significance of the tribe’s way of life.

Each artifact holds a story of its own, representing the values, traditions, and craftsmanship that have been passed down through generations. The items displayed here have been carefully curated to highlight the diverse aspects of the tribe’s history.
